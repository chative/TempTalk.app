Chative is a security & privacy focused chat app.

We, humans, have natural ways to communicate. We say things to each other in private, then we forget over time. Chative is here to do that digitally.

Chative is fully encrypted end-to-end. All your messages are fully encrypted in-transit or at rest.
Chative does not store your messages, not on the server side, and not on the client side.
Chative does not store metadata. Once a message is delivered, no other info about the message is kept.
Chative is open-source from Signal. We believe secure apps should be transparent in how they work.
You can invite or connect with friends with a unique link or QR code.
Chative does not require a phone number. You can register on Chative with an email address.
